# For Business Owners

## The Challenge
Running a real estate media company means juggling photographers, managing quality, invoicing clients, and growing the business—often with spreadsheets and disconnected tools.

## How We Solve It

### Unified Operations Dashboard
- **Single view** of all active jobs, their status, and who's handling them
- **Real-time updates** as photographers complete shoots and editors finish processing
- **Bottleneck alerts** when jobs are stuck or behind schedule

### Automated Workflows
| Trigger | What Happens Automatically |
|---------|---------------------------|
| Booking confirmed | Photographer assigned, calendar blocked, reminder emails scheduled |
| Photos uploaded | HDR processing starts, QC queue populated |
| QC approved | Delivery page created, client notified, review request queued |
| Payment received | Invoice marked paid, QuickBooks synced |

### Financial Insights
- **Revenue tracking** by service type, package, and photographer
- **Profit margins** per job with cost breakdown
- **Lapsed client alerts** when regular customers haven't booked recently
- **Weekly summaries** delivered to your inbox

### AI-Powered Content
- **Listing descriptions** generated from photos and property data
- **Social media posts** ready to share for each delivery
- **Marketing campaigns** with drip email sequences
- **Video slideshows** created automatically from photos

### Team Management
- **Territory mapping** for photographer assignments
- **Skill tracking** to match photographers to job requirements
- **Performance metrics** for each team member
- **Automated scheduling** based on availability and location

# How It All Works Together

## The Complete Workflow

```
Agent Books → Photographer Assigned → Shoot Completed → QC Review → Delivery Ready
     ↓              ↓                      ↓              ↓            ↓
  Auto-email    Calendar sync         Auto-upload     Quick review   Client notified
  Reminders     Route planned         HDR processing  Standards      Downloads ready
  Agent portal  Weather check         AI enhancement  Consistency    Seller proofing
```

## A Day in the Life

### 9:00 AM - Booking Comes In
- Agent books through portal
- System assigns photographer based on territory and availability
- Photographer receives notification with property details
- Agent gets confirmation with estimated delivery time

### 10:30 AM - Shoot Begins
- Photographer opens Shoot Mode on tablet
- Checks shot list for property type
- Verifies airspace for drone work
- Sends en-route notification to agent

### 12:00 PM - Shoot Complete
- Photos upload automatically during drive to next property
- HDR processing begins immediately
- QC queue updated with new job

### 2:00 PM - QC Review
- Specialist reviews photos in kanban interface
- Approves 45 photos, requests 2 retouches
- Approved photos move to delivery prep
- Photographer notified of needed corrections

### 3:30 PM - Delivery Ready
- Delivery page automatically generated
- Agent receives email with link
- Seller proofing invitation sent (if enabled)
- Social media content generated
- Review request scheduled for 48 hours later

### 5:00 PM - Business Owner Review
- Dashboard shows 8 jobs completed today
- Revenue tracking updated
- Weekly summary preparing for Friday delivery
- Lapsed client alert for agent who hasn't booked in 60 days
